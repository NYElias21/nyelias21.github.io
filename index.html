Lyric Up!

Think fast and sing faster! Challenge friends to find song lyrics containing displayed words. Multiple game modes, competitive fun, and musical creativity in one app!

Party Mode

Compete with 2-4 players locally! Think fast and tap when you know a matching lyric.

Word Rush

Take turns finding lyrics in this casual, turn-based mode.

Customize Your Game

Adjust timer settings and toggle sound/vibration in the Settings menu.

How to Play:

  • Choose your game mode and gather your friends
  • Watch for words that appear on screen
  • Think of a song lyric containing that word
  • Tap your zone when you know a lyric
  • Sing your lyric to score points
  • Have fun and get creative!

Perfect for parties, family game nights, or casual fun with friends!
